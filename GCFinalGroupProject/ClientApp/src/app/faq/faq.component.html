How does Dualverse: Heroes work?

Upon registering an account with Dualverse, the player is randomly generated three heroes from the Superhero API database to choose from.

The player is then prompted to the Igor's Shop to purchase any items they wish in order to assist them in battle.

Should the player win, they earn credits that can be used to buy higher priced items in the Shop. The player also begins a consecutive win streak. After 10 consecutive wins, the player encounters a "boss" character.

Should the player lose, their hero dies and they are generated a new one at random.

How does the Battle System work?

Each hero in our collection comes with a set of powerstats that is made up of six attributes: Intelligence, Strength, Speed, Durability, Power, and Combat.

Dualverse: Heroes is a turn based battle game. The first hero to attack is determined by their speed.

Each hero has a certain amount of Hitpoints and Damage they can deal. These are calculated by the following formulas:

Battle System Calculations
Damage (STR x 1.3) + (POW x 1.5) + COM
Hitpoints (DUR x 5) + (SP / 2) + (INT / 2)

Items purchased at Igor's Shop, such as a Health Kit, can add to your total Hitpoints or Damage.

The player continues battling until either their hero or the opponent runs out of Hitpoints.
